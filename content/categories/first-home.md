Buying your first home is not one decision — it&rsquo;s about forty. Arrivau&rsquo;s first-home hub breaks them down into five stages:

1. **Savings** — how much deposit you actually need in each state
2. **Grants** — FHOG state-by-state, plus First Home Guarantee Scheme (5% deposit, no LMI)
3. **Pre-approval** — what lenders need, in what order
4. **Offer and contract** — cooling off, conditional vs unconditional, what the agent doesn&rsquo;t volunteer
5. **Settlement** — final inspection, day-of funds transfer, insurance

Start at [the FHOG state table](/first-home-owner-grant-australia-guide/) and work down the list. All the articles are linked so you can jump between them without losing your place.
