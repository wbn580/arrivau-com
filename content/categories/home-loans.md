Home loans are the reason most people meet a broker. The mortgage market in Australia is dominated by four majors (CBA, Westpac, ANZ, NAB) but the real negotiating leverage sits with the 300+ smaller lenders competing for your file.

## What you&rsquo;ll find in this section

- **Rate tables** — refreshed monthly against lender sheet rates
- **Product explainers** — fixed vs variable, offset, redraw, split loans, low-doc
- **Scenarios** — first home, refinance, investment, self-employed, non-resident
- **Case studies** — anonymised real files showing what actually got approved

## Where to start

If this is your first time thinking about a home loan, begin with [the rate comparison page](/home-loans/) and [the borrowing power calculator](/calculators/borrowing-power/). If you already have a loan and want to know whether to refinance, run [the refinance calculator](/calculators/refinance-cashback/) and read the refinance case studies.
