Property investment in Australia is a reasonable way to build wealth over 10–15 years — and a ferociously bad way to make money in under five. This section is where we put investor-specific content.

## What we cover

- **Yield vs growth** — the trade-off, and what actually pays down the loan
- **Portfolio structure** — individual, joint, trust, SMSF
- **Land tax** — state thresholds and how aggregation works
- **Refinance cycles** — when to pull equity, when not to
- **Exit planning** — CGT, staged disposal, generational transfer
